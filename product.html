Transforming Ideas into Products with Dell Technologies.

At IndiLogic, we take pride in delivering top quality products and services that exceed industry standards. Our collabrations with Dell Technologies as trusted strategic partner has resulted in products that are used by over 16+ million users worldwide.

PRODUCTS

Dell Peripheral Manager

  • Customization tool: Supports personalization of Dell-branded peripherals like webcams, mice, and keyboards.
  • Device management: Allows users to manage devices and update firmware easily.
  • Wide user base: Approximately 8 million active installations worldwide.
  • Pre-installed on Dell machines: Available straight from the factory for immediate setup.
  • Empowerment: Gives users control to tweak settings and enhance their tech experience.
  • Passion for innovation: Driven by the impact on millions of users, fueling continuous improvements.

Dell Pair

  • Seamless connectivity: Facilitates effortless pairing of Dell Bluetooth devices like wireless mice and keyboards.
  • Automatic detection: Dell Pair automatically detects and prompts pairing with any Dell Bluetooth device within range.
  • Wide availability: Installed on several Dell platforms, pre-installed on select machines.
  • Large-scale adoption: Approximately 20 million factory installations each year.
  • Enhanced user experience: Focuses on making daily tech interactions smoother and more intuitive.
  • Innovation-driven: Team is committed to improving user experiences through continuous innovation.

Dell Secure Link Gen-3 Dongle

  • Enhanced security and performance: Designed to improve wireless connections for Dell RF solutions.
  • Replaces existing hardware: The Gen-3 Dongle replaces the Dell Universal Receiver RF Dongle across all Dell RF devices.
  • Global impact: Distributed to numerous Dell vendors worldwide.
  • Large-scale distribution: Expected to reach around 30 million units annually.
  • Focus on data protection: A crucial upgrade to safeguard user data and improve device efficiency.
  • Innovation-driven: Provided seamless plug-and-play functionality, enabling the pairing of multiple compatible devices with a single dongle.
