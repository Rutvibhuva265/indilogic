Firmware/IoT Development

We design and develop bespoke firmware for a variety of microcontroller architectures and families, ensuring that your specific hardware requirements are met with precision. Our expertise spans across popular platforms including Nordic, STMicroelectronics, Intel, NXP Coldfire, Kinetis, Renesas, Microchip, Atmel, Qualcomm, and any Linux/Android-based microcontroller.

We adapt to the unique needs of each platform, delivering firmware that drives your hardware to its full potential while maintaining highstandards of reliability and efficiency.

Embedded Firmware Development

We offer comprehensive embedded firmware development services, including both low-level and high-level firmware as well as subsystems to drive your embedded systems.

Our firmware solutions are designed to integrate seamlessly with hardware and microprocessors from leading companies such as NVIDIA, Qualcomm, Intel, ARM, and others.

Legacy Firmware Migration

As a specialized firmware development company, we facilitate the migration of your firmware from one MCU vendor to another. We handle all aspects of the transition, including re-layout of the PCB design if necessary, to ensure that your firmware is fully portable and optimized for the new hardware.

Our expertise ensures a smooth transition with minimal disruption,enabling your systems to leverage the latest technology while preserving functionality and performance.
