ABOUT US

At IndiLogic LLC (USA), we are committed to delivering high-quality products and services to our clients.

Established in 2012 in Texas, USA, we have dedicated years to achieving excellence in Windows application development, embedded applications, and firmware development. As a privately funded and privately owned company, we have experienced exponential growth, generating multimillion-dollar revenues.

Mehul Patel (CEO and CTO)

Bringing over 25 years of leadership experience in the tech industry. With a strong background in engineering, business development, and strategic planning, Mehul has successfully led IndiLogic for decades. His visionary leadership is dedicated to driving IndiLogic to new heights

His leadership has been instrumental in developing IndiLogic's flagship products, ensuring they are robust, reliable, and future-ready. Outside of work, Mehul is an avid coder who enjoys mentoring young engineers. He firmly believes in the power of technology to change the world and is continually exploring new ways to innovate and inspire the next generation of tech leaders
